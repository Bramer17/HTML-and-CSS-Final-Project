Stress Relief Ideas

When stress overwhelms your nervous system, your body is flooded with chemicals that prepare you for fight or flight. This stress response can be lifesaving in emergency situations where you need to act quickly. But when it is constantly activated by the stresses of everyday life, it can wear your body down and take a toll on your emotional and physical health. This website includes many different methods to relieve stress such as yoga, calming music, and helpful apps.
